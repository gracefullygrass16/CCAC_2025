“The first and best victory is to conquer self” —Plato
Every good leader starts with a deep understanding of themselves. With this quiz, we invite you to embark on a journey of self-discovery that will reveal your unique leadership qualities:
Your Strengths: What makes you stand out as a leader? Learn how to harness these qualities to inspire and guide others effectively.
Areas For Growth: Identify the skills or traits you can develop to become a more balanced and well-rounded leader.
Self vs. Peer Perception: Discover how your peers view your leadership qualities compared to how you see yourself. This will help you identify blind spots and areas where you excel.
Leadership is a journey of continuous growth and self-discovery, and this quiz is more than just a snapshot—it’s a tool for continuous improvement.
By taking this quiz at the start of your leadership journey and revisiting it later, you can track your progress and growth over time. The insights you gain will help you not only to grow as a leader but also to build stronger relationships and make a meaningful impact within your team.
